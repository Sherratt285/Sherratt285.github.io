What is your long-term goal and/or career pathway?



I am working towards front end or full stack web development. I have studied some back end using C++, and am familiar with databases. I also love aviation so I may attempt to get into the aerospace industry at some ponit.

A description of your own strengths and limitations in relation to learning



One of the things I have noticed is I struggle to stay motivated with arbitrary goals. I learn best when I am working on a project that is aiming to solve a problem. I am at the point where I can comfortably reference language documentation for syntax when needed. When I am just learning coding without building a project, I easilly get bored. However I am also the type of person who can very easily spend too long trying to figure out a problem, so I need to learn to see when something is good enough, so I can continue to move on and not get bogged down with details and specifics.
